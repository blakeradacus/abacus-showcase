Assumptions + methodology

~250K themed drink purchases
350 stores × ~250 drinks/day avg × 14 days ≈ 1.2M total transactions. Themed drinks conservatively estimated at 10–20% of orders ≈ 175K–350K. Midpoint used: ~250K. This is modeled — KFT has not provided transaction data. Each purchase receives a QR code on the cup.
50K TCG promo cards
Confirmed production figure. ~143 cards per store across 350+ locations. Distributed while supplies last — independent of the QR unlock, which appears on every cup.
~22K Pudgy World new users
~250K eligible purchases × ~9% estimated QR redemption rate. Conservative benchmark: QR-linked GWP campaigns in CPG/F&B typically see 1–5% scan-to-redeem. Upside at 5% ≈ 12.5K users. Downside at 1% ≈ 2.5K. The +147% headline uses the 22K midpoint against confirmed 15K DAU.
